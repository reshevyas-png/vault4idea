Questions? We Have Answers

How is this different from /export?

Export gives you files scattered everywhere. 💡 Idea Vault keeps everything searchable and indexed. Plus, prompt caching means restored conversations cost 90% less. Export costs full price every time.

What happens to my saves if I stop paying?

Your local saves stay forever. Pro cloud saves are accessible for 30 days after cancellation, then deleted. You can download everything before that.

How does prompt caching actually work?

When you save, we create a cache key. When you restore, Claude's API uses that key instead of reprocessing tokens. You pay 25% extra on first use, then 90% less on restores. It's built into the API—we automate it.

Is my data encrypted?

Yes. Local saves use SQLite on your machine. Cloud syncs are encrypted in transit and at rest via Supabase. We don't see your conversations.

Can I use this offline?

Yes! All saves work offline. Auto-save syncs when you reconnect. Cloud features require internet.

Can I export my data later?

Absolutely. One-click export to JSON, Markdown, or PDF. Your data is always yours.
